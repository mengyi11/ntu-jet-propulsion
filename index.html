About Us

Hi! We are the Jet Propulsion Club (JPC) — the very first rocket and propulsion club at NTU. Our members are driven by curiosity, collaboration, and the thrill of flight. From design and simulation to fabrication and launch, we aim to push the boundaries of student rocketry in Singapore.

Our Teams

Propulsion

Team members in Propulsion deal with the heart of the vehicle and that is the motor, as well as other devices which deals with pressure. This sub-team is responsible in making sure our rocket propels forward with all it's might as well go through seperation for parachute deployment.

Structures

This team deals with the skeleton of the rocket and that would primarly be the airframe. They would have to design, simulate and test the rocket while making sure the objectives are met.

Avionics

This team deals with the electrical side of things and gives life to the components of our rocket. These members design electrical circuits and decide the avionics components our rocket would need.

Payload

This is where innovation is driven. This team is incharge of building our CANSATs while meeting competition goals. This team could consist of multiple disciplines, from electrical, mechanical to biology to meet the needs of our CANSATs.

Admin

The Admin Team is incharge of outreach, logistics and publicity. With the significant works of members from the Admin Team, NTU JPC is able to put itself on the map and make things happen.

Finance

The Finance team deal with finance management as well as strategies to acquire funding to keep the club's ventures going.
